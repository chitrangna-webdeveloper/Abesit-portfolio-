ABESIT, as I actually see it

First-year CSE, batch 2026–2030 — the best and worst of campus life so far.

A quick look around campus

The good stuff

Approachable faculty
Doubt sessions, project help, career advice — most professors genuinely make time for it.

Fest season energy
Tech fests and hackathons bring the whole campus alive — best time of the year, hands down.

Improving infrastructure
Newer labs and library upgrades are a visible step up from what seniors describe.

The friends you find
First year is chaotic, but the people you end up with make it all worth it.

The real talk

Wi-Fi is a gamble
Fine in some blocks, gone in others — usually right when you need it most.

Canteen food is inconsistent
Some days decent, some days a mystery. Maggi remains the only safe bet.

Strict attendance rule
The 75% rule is enforced without much room for "just this once."

The daily commute
Traffic near the NH makes the last stretch feel longer than the rest of the journey.
